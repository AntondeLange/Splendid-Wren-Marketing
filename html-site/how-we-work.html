HOW WE WORK

At Splendid Wren Marketing, how we work is just as important as what we deliver. We take the time to listen, get to know you, get to know your brand, develop strategy and give you the tools to thrive.

WHAT SETS US APART

Most marketing support still relies on scattered tools, outsourced help, or generic AI that doesn’t really understand your brand. Splendid Wren Marketing is different — we combine clear strategy and human-designed foundations with a purpose-built AI 'marketing toolkit' designed specifically for your business, so your marketing can happen in one place.

Our online, AI-powered toolkit takes the guesswork out of your marketing. It is a collection of practical, time-saving tools designed to simplify your marketing function and help you move from ideas to action — faster. Inside, you’ll find tools like our:

  • Brand Audit - an honest overview of your current brand status and key improvements
  • 60-minute Marketing Planner - a six-month marketing strategy that gives you a simple blueprint and content plan for your brand
  • Campaign Planning Tool - for when your need a strategy, ideas and a plan for high-impact marketing activity
  • VA Brief Builder - a fully developed brief to help you get the best results from your marketing or social media virtual assistant

The Splendid Wren Marketing Toolkit is always expanding, delivering on client and community demand. Each tool is designed to support real small business life with clear steps, smart structure, and a human-first approach.

WORKING WITH SPLENDID WREN MARKETING

When you work with Splendid Wren Marketing you work with a human first. For packages Foundation and above we have a four step process:

01

Informal chat

Let's get to know one another! We have a conversation to get clear on the heart of your brand — who you are, what you stand for, who you’re here for, and why it matters. This is where the noise fades and the real story starts.

02

Shape the strategy

Next, we tap into the Splendid Wren Marketing Toolkit to turn that clarity into a simple strategy. We define what you need to say, where you should show up, and what actions will move your business forward (without trying to do everything at once).

03

Put into practice

Then we make it practical. We have a suite of AI tools that can translate your strategy into real-world marketing — messaging, content direction, priorities, and more.

04

Momentum building

Finally, we help you build confidence and consistency over time. We refine what’s working, adjust what isn’t, and strengthen your brand foundations so growth feels steady, sustainable, and aligned.
