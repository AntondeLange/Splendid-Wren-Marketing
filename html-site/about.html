ABOUT

Splendid Wren Marketing was created from a simple belief: small businesses deserve brands that feel clear, confident and truly their own.

Founder Story

Meet Sarah Moore. After more than 20 years of working alongside countless small business owners, it became clear to Sarah that many weren't struggling because they lacked ideas or ambition — they were overwhelmed by noise. Endless advice, constant notifications and fast-moving trends that promised quick wins. It was just easier to ignore marketing.

But the thing is, marketing is easy - especially when you have a small and agile brand. It's just about getting the foundations in place so technology can do the heavy lifting.

Over the past 2 years, Sarah has become really interested in AI. The journey began by creating tools at home that could help friends simplify their marketing and enjoy what got them into business in the first place. It turned out that this AI 'tinkering' was her passion. So, she left her stable, well-paying corporate career to join a growing number of small business owners - and hasn't regretted a moment of it.

OUR PURPOSE

We believe that a business doesn’t need to be big to deserve a brand that stands out.

At Splendid Wren Marketing our purpose is to bring clarity, confidence and meaning to the small business people who are building something real. When a business understands who they truly are, everything else becomes possible. We show up with optimism, insight, innovation and honesty, helping our clients uncover the heart of their brand and show them how they can use modern tools to express their unique personality with purpose.

Most of all, we’re here to help small business owners feel seen, supported and inspired — so their brands can thrive in a world that moves fast, but still values the real, the heartfelt and the brilliantly human.

Why Splendid Wren?

The name Splendid Wren is a reflection of what we believe great brands are made of. The Splendid Wren (also known as a blue wren) is small but striking — vibrant, confident and impossible to overlook when you spy one in its natural environment. It’s a reminder that a business doesn’t need to be the loudest or biggest to be heard, only clear, consistent and true to itself. That idea sits at the heart of everything we do: helping small business brands show up with confidence, clarity and humanity in a world that can be noisy and cluttered.
